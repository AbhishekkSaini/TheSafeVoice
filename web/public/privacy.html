Privacy Policy

Last updated:

TheSafeVoice respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info (email, optional display name, phone)
  • Content you post (stories, comments)
  • Security and usage data (IP, device, analytics)
  • Optional location for SOS features

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and secure the service
  • Moderate content to keep users safe
  • Notify you about important changes

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data with service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) under strict agreements and when required by law.

4. Your Choices

  • Update or delete your account
  • Control what you share publicly or anonymously
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

5. Security

We use best practices to protect your data. No system is 100% secure; report issues to security@safevoice.community.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy: privacy@safevoice.community

Women’s Safety Topics

Essential steps to follow during different types of emergencies

Harassment Prevention

  • Recognize red flags and trust your instincts
  • Set boundaries and use de-escalation strategies
  • Document incidents safely
  • Reach out for support in the forum

Legal Help

  • Know your rights and reporting options
  • Find local legal helplines and NGOs
  • Steps to file a complaint safely
  • Preserve evidence responsibly

Personal Safety Planning

  • Share live location with trusted contacts
  • Build a safety circle and check-in plan
  • Use code words and safe routes
  • Keep important contacts/records accessible
